# 🚀 Professor Quick Start

Welcome! Professor helps you **learn by doing** — not by reading explanations.

## Your First 5 Minutes

The entire Professor workflow boils down to **3 simple steps**:

---

### Step 1: Create Your Course

Say something like:

> **"I want to learn [topic]"**

I'll ask you 4 quick questions about your background and goals, then create a personalized course with a syllabus just for you.

---

### Step 2: Load a Section

Run:

> **/professor:next**

This loads your first section with:
- Clear concept explanation
- Real-world use case
- Hands-on exercise

**Do the exercise yourself.** Don't ask me to write it for you — that defeats the purpose.

---

### Step 3: Get Reviewed

Run:

> **/professor:done**

I'll review your work using **Socratic questions** — asking you to explain your thinking, rather than rewriting your code. If you understand it well, the section marks complete.

---

## The Learning Loop

```
[new topic] → [next] → [try exercise] → [done] → [next] → ...
                    ↑__________________|
```

Repeat steps 2–3 for each section until all sections are done. Then your **capstone project** unlocks.

---

## Quick Reference

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `professor:next` | Load the next section |
| `professor:done` | Get reviewed, mark section complete |
| `professor:hint` | Get help when stuck (3 layers) |
| `professor:discuss` | Ask concept questions |
| `professor:progress` | See how far you've come |

---

## Pro Tips

- **Stuck?** → `/professor:hint` for layered hints
- **Need help with a concept?** → `/professor:discuss`
- **Off track?** → `/professor:progress` to see where you are
- **Finish all sections?** → Your capstone project unlocks!
